Beef Shami Kabab

  • Ingredients
  • Instruction
  • Serving









Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas), soaked and cooked
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil for frying





Instruction

  1. In a large bowl, combine the minced beef,


  2. Cooked chana dal,


  3. Chopped onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, coriander powder, garam masala, salt, and fresh coriander leaves.

  4. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly until well combined. You can use your hands to ensure everything is evenly mixed.

  5. Divide the mixture into small portions and shape each portion into a flat, round patty (kabab shape).

  6. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.

  7. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the kababs in the pan
    Fry the kababs for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

  8. Remove the kababs from the pan and place them on paper towels to drain any excess oil.

  • Serve the beef shami kababs hot with mint chutney or yogurt sauce.
